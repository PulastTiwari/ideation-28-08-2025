The Core Idea: LLM-Powered Reasoning

Our innovation lies in leveraging a Large Language Model (LLM) for classification. Instead of training a model on numerical data, we convert traffic statistics into a human-readable prompt, allowing the LLM to perform a reasoning-based analysis. This approach is more flexible and interpretable than traditional models.
